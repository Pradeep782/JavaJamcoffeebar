Follow the Winding Road to JavaJam

We're a little out of way, but take a drive in the country down Garrett Bay Road to JavaJam today! Indulge in our locally roasted free-trade coffee and home-made pastries. You'll feel right at home at JavaJam!

Friendly and ecletic - JavaJam Coffee Bar is the perfect place to take a break, enjoy a refreshing beerage, and have a snack or light meal.

JavaJam Coffee Bar features:

  • Organic Salads
  • Music and Poetry Readings
